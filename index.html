About Bubble

Bubble is a next-generation compressed-air energy storage (CAES) system engineered to provide long-duration, high-efficiency renewable firming. It stores air at 200 bar and temperatures exceeding 1000 °C, delivering exceptional energy density and life expectancy.

Developed in collaboration with TNO (Rijswijk Geo-Energy Facility), Bubble is advancing toward TRL-6 validation with pilot execution planned before mid-2026.
